My Other Hobbies


Editing

I use the computer application Adobe Photoshop when I want to edit photos from certain events or for clients.
During transportation, I tend to pass time by editing random pictures I have in my cameraroll with a phone application called PicsArt

Drawing

From time to time, after a long and hard day, I like to just sit down, put on some headphones, turn on my music. And with a piece of paper and a pencil at my desk. I just go at it, I draw either whatever comes with the flow of my mood or new ideas of pictures that I can take (the angles, the poses, the size of photo, etc.) I usually draw them in a manga form since it is the only drawing style that I am able to draw.

Hairstyling

I work part-time as a barber at my cousin's barbershop. I tend to enjoy grooming hair since it creates a sense of art on the human body. It gives character to a person's figure or personality. And the sound of the clippers is one of the most satisfying sounds there can possibly exist.

Climbing

I have done some photoshoot sessions in places like inside a forest or in the mountains and that, sometimes, involve climbing trees and rocks. The higher I go, the prettier the view of everything around me gets. Sometimes, I can get the best view of a sunset within the clouds.

Night drives

The only way to cool off from anything making you angry or upset, the only way to reach a nice and calm vybe, the only feel the wind flow through you, the only way to forget about everything about life is a simple drive at night with some chill music. Sometimes, its a nice feeling when you drive during a sunset, but its more effective when it is during the night.
